About Us

A family-owned business of contractors in Montreal. Our expertise is passed on from one generation to the next.

Founded in 1986 by Orlando Filippone Sr. with sons Joe and Tony, O.M.F began as a snow removal business, and has since branched out to include services in Excavation, Asphalt, Demolition, Landscaping, Sewer assistance and Cement work. Today O.M.F continues to be a family affair, merging experience, passion, as well as professional expertise in order to guarantee customer satisfaction.

Les Entrepreneurs O.M.F holds a RBQ license and is fully recognized by CCQ (Commission de la construction de Quebec) and CSST (Commission des Normes, de la Santé et de la Sécurité du Travail).
